DATA PRIVACY PROTOCOL ×

01 // Zero-Knowledge Architecture

We employ a Zero-Knowledge framework where all estate telemetry is encrypted at the source using AES-256 military-grade standards. This ensures that sensitive operational data is accessible only by authorized client personnel and is never visible to third-party providers or system administrators.

02 // TLS 1.3 Transport Security

All data in transit utilizes TLS 1.3 encryption protocols, providing a fortified layer of protection against interception during the high-frequency transmission of statutory safety and compliance telemetry from your estate to the secure cloud.

03 // Data Minimisation & Sovereignty

Safe Compliance operates strictly under UK Data Protection laws. We process only the specific data points essential for statutory safety governance. No metadata or estate analytics are transmitted to third-party providers or stored outside of Tier-3 UK Data Centers.

04 // Biometric & Hardware Authentication

We operate on a strict 'Need-to-Know' basis. Access to the secure privacy ledger is gated by biometric or hardware-token authenticated sessions, ensuring the integrity of the audit trail remains absolute even in the event of credential compromise.

05 // Sovereign Isolated Processing

Unlike standard SaaS platforms, our processing nodes are logically isolated. Your estate analytics never leave the UK jurisdiction, maintaining strict adherence to DPA 2018 and UK GDPR standards for total enterprise sovereignty.

01 // Data Integrity Protocol

Our architecture employs multi-node replication across Tier-3 UK data centres. We guarantee 99.99% uptime for all compliance dashboards, with real-time failover protocols in place to prevent data loss or service interruption during critical reporting periods.

03 // Sovereign Data Residency

All processing and storage nodes are located strictly within the UK. We do not offshore estate telemetry or metadata, ensuring your sensitive operational data remains under the absolute protection of UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

04 // Secure Stewardship & NIST Compliance

We perform rigorous monthly governance audits to ensure our security controls exceed the requirements of the NIST Cybersecurity Framework. Every system interaction, administrative change, and data access request is logged via a forensic audit trail for total enterprise transparency.

05 // Zero-Trust Access Control

Our governance model utilizes a Zero-Trust architecture. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) and hardware-token verification are mandatory for all administrative ledger adjustments, ensuring no single point of failure in data access.

06 // Automated Disposal Protocol

Automated data purging at the end-of-lifecycle ensures no legacy liability for the estate owner. We utilize cryptographic erasure (Crypto-Erase) for all hardware decommissioning, guaranteeing that retired data is permanently unrecoverable.
